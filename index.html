Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reschedule an appointment?

Yes. You can reschedule your appointment for one-time only.

Can I get refunds if my session didn't happen?

There are no refunds. Refunds are only possible if an Edviser has cancelled the meeting after two reschedules. Refunds will take 14 business days after resolution.

How to pick the right Edviser?

Our Edvisers have mentioned about the area of guidance and counselling. You can book an Edviser that aligns with your future goals.

Can I visit the office for counselling?

We don't provide counselling in office. Many of our Edvisers are not from Pakistan.

What If I want to take help in my applications?

You can contact us for assitance in your university applications after counselling.

Can I reach out to Edviser after first session?

Yes. You can reach out if Edviser has recommended you any actionable steps and you need evaluations. However, you would need to book the Edviser again for next session.
